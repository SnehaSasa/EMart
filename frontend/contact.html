Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Our standard delivery time is 24-48 hours depending on your vicinity to our nearest branch.

Do you accept international payments?

Currently, we only process domestic cards within the US, but we accept global digital wallets.

What is the return policy?

Apparel can be returned within 30 days. Groceries and perishable provisions are non-refundable.
